The Scout explains the basics of the Wildcard chip for novice managers in 2024/25[Fantasy Premier League](https://fantasy.premierleague.com/).
The Wildcard chip is the most powerful of the five chips available to managers as it allows you unlimited free transfers to overhaul your entire squad without incurring any points hits.
[ Fantasy Premier League  FPL experts: When should you use your Wildcard?  ](https://www.premierleague.com/news/4106422 "FPL experts: When should you use your Wildcard?") The Wildcard chip can be used twice a season. The first Wildcard will be available from the start of this season until Sunday 29 December 13:30 GMT. The second Wildcard will be available afterSunday 29 December 13:30 GMT in readiness for the January transfer window opening and remains available until the end of the season. 
The Wildcard chip is played when confirming transfers and can't be cancelled once played.
When to play your Wildcard is very much a team-dependant decision. 
When should you use your Wildcard?
If you have suffered a poor start to 2024/25 and don’t own either Erling Haaland(£15.2m) or Mohamed Salah (£12.6m) – who are the top-scoring players after the first three Gameweeks – then there is plenty of upside in using it now.
Both Haaland and Salah’s potential for huge captaincy hauls means you need to have at least one of them in your line-up to avoid falling further down the overall rankings.

A club’s displays under a new head coach can be another reason to reassess plans for your squad.
If you were cautious over loading up on [Liverpool](https://www.premierleague.com/clubs/10/Liverpool/overview) players in your opening squad after Jurgen Klopp departed last season, you could now reverse that decision with a Wildcard. 
The Reds have thrived under Arne Slot and are the only side to keep a clean sheet in all three of the opening Gameweeks, with Trent Alexander-Arnold ’s (£7.0m) huge attacking potential making him the best option. Meanwhile, Luis Diaz and Diogo Jota(both £7.6m) provide strong double-up picks alongside Salah in attack. 
Using the Wildcard now – during an international break – can also help lift your squad value, as players are more likely to change in cost when there is a two-week gap between Gameweek deadlines. Historically, players have risen or fallen in price by a maximum of £0.3m per Gameweek.

Keep an eye out for Gameweek 6
Assessing the schedules for each club can also help managers identify the best time to play their Wildcard.
For example, if you don’t own any [Arsenal](https://www.premierleague.com/clubs/1/Arsenal/overview) attackers, then Gameweek 6 could be the ideal time to use the chip to bring in Bukayo Saka(£10.1m) and Kai Havertz(£8.1m).
The Gunners have tough matches against [Tottenham Hotspur](https://www.premierleague.com/clubs/21/Tottenham-Hotspur/overview) and [Manchester City](https://www.premierleague.com/clubs/11/Manchester-City/overview) in the next two Gameweeks.
But from Gameweek 6, they have back-to-back home matches with promoted pair [Leicester City](https://www.premierleague.com/clubs/26/Leicester-City/overview) and [Southampton](https://www.premierleague.com/clubs/20/Southampton/overview), followed by a visit to [AFC Bournemouth](https://www.premierleague.com/clubs/127/Bournemouth/overview).
This window could be particularly appealing if you have loaded up on [Newcastle United](https://www.premierleague.com/clubs/23/Newcastle-United/overview) assets such as Anthony Gordon(£7.4m) and Alexander Isak (£8.5m) in your opening squad. The Magpies host Man City in Gameweek 6 and will then face [Chelsea](https://www.premierleague.com/clubs/4/Chelsea/overview) and Arsenal in their subsequent four fixtures. 
Typically, the second Wildcard is best to save until late in the season to profit from any Double Gameweeks (DGWs). A DGW is when a club has two matches in a Gameweek and is usually caused by postponements due to domestic cup matches.

## Carta di Identità della Ricetta
  * **183,2 KCal** Calorie per porzione
  * Difficoltà [facile](https://www.my-personaltrainer.it/Tv/Ricette/Difficolta/2.html "Ricette facile")
  * Dosi per [6 persone](https://www.my-personaltrainer.it/Tv/Ricette/Persone/6persone.html "Ricette per 6 persone")
  * Preparazione [20 minuti](https://www.my-personaltrainer.it/Tv/Ricette/Preparazione/20minuti.html "Ricette con preparazione di circa 20 minuti")
  * Costo [medio](https://www.my-personaltrainer.it/Tv/Ricette/Costo/medio.html "Ricette a costo medio")
  * **Nota** 20 minuti per la preparazione; 40-45 minuti di cottura


Categoria Ricetta:  [Dolci e Dessert](https://www.my-personaltrainer.it/Tv/Ricette/Dolci_Dessert.html) / Torta alla Ricotta - Senza Farina 
## Ingredienti
  * 550 gr di [ricotta fresca](https://www.my-personaltrainer.it/Tv/Ricette/Ingredienti/ricotta_fresca.html "Ricette con ricotta fresca")
  * 3 di [uova](https://www.my-personaltrainer.it/Tv/Ricette/Ingredienti/uova.html "Ricette con uova")
  * 120 gr di [zucchero di canna](https://www.my-personaltrainer.it/Tv/Ricette/Ingredienti/zucchero_di_canna.html "Ricette con zucchero di canna")
  * 100 gr di [uvetta sultanina](https://www.my-personaltrainer.it/Tv/Ricette/Ingredienti/uvetta_sultanina.html "Ricette con uvetta sultanina")
  * 100 gr di [farina di riso](https://www.my-personaltrainer.it/Tv/Ricette/Ingredienti/farina_di_riso.html "Ricette con farina di riso")
  * succo e scorza di [limone non trattato](https://www.my-personaltrainer.it/Tv/Ricette/Ingredienti/limone_non_trattato.html "Ricette con limone non trattato")


Per guarnire
  * (frutti rossi) quanto basta di [marmellata](https://www.my-personaltrainer.it/Tv/Ricette/Ingredienti/marmellata.html "Ricette con marmellata")


## Materiale Occorrente
  * Fruste elettriche
  * Ciotola
  * Bilancina pesa [alimenti](https://www.my-personaltrainer.it/alimenti.html "Alimenti e Prodotti alimentari")
  * Carta da forno
  * Teglia a cerniera dal diametro di 18 cm


## Preparazione
  * In una ciotola, raccogliere le [uova](https://www.my-personaltrainer.it/nutrizione/uova.html "Uova e salute") insieme allo [zucchero](https://www.my-personaltrainer.it/zucchero.html "Zucchero"): lavorare il composto a crema per 5 minuti, fino a quando risulterà spumoso e soffice.
  * Nel frattempo, ammorbidire l'uvetta in una ciotolina con acqua tiepida, per 5 minuti.
  * A questo punto, versare la ricotta all'interno della crema di uova, un po' per volta, per evitare che il composto si sleghi.
  * Aromatizzare la crema di ricotta e uova con succo e scorza di [limone](https://www.my-personaltrainer.it/erboristeria/limone.html "Limone in Erboristeria: Proprietà del Limone"), ed essenza di [vaniglia](https://www.my-personaltrainer.it/alimentazione/vaniglia.html "Vaniglia").
  * Scolare l'uvetta, strizzarla dall'ammollo e farla rotolare nella [farina](https://www.my-personaltrainer.it/alimentazione/proprieta-nutrizionali-della-semola-di-grano-duro-e-differenze-dalla-farina-di-grano-tenero.html "Proprietà nutrizionali della semola di grano duro e differenze dalla farina di grano tenero") [di riso](https://www.my-personaltrainer.it/nutrizione/olio-di-riso.html "Olio di Riso") prima di unirla all'impasto.


### Lo sapevi che...
L'aggiunta di un po' di [farina di riso](https://www.my-personaltrainer.it/alimentazione/farina-di-riso.html "Farina di Riso") insieme all'uvetta è un consiglio utile per evitare che l'uvetta [affondi](https://www.my-personaltrainer.it/allenamento/affondi-frontali-manubri-sul-posto-come-eseguono-benefici-errori-non-commettere.html "Affondi frontali con manubri sul posto: come si eseguono, benefici e errori da non commettere") sulla base del dolce.
  * Versare l'impasto all'interno di uno stampo a cerniera dal diametro di 18 cm, dunque cuocere in forno ventilato a 160°C per 40-45 minuti. Lasciar completamente raffreddare.


### Qualche alternativa
Per arricchire il dolce, si possono aggiungere delle scorze di [frutta candita](https://www.my-personaltrainer.it/alimentazione/frutta-candita.html "Frutta Candita: Nutrizione, Dieta e Cucina"), dei pezzi di [cioccolato](https://www.my-personaltrainer.it/alimentazione/il-cioccolato-fa-bene-o-fa-male-benefici-e-proprieta.html "Il cioccolato fa bene o fa male? Benefici e proprietà") oppure semplice [marmellata](https://www.my-personaltrainer.it/alimentazione/marmellata.html "Marmellata e confettura") agli [agrumi](https://www.my-personaltrainer.it/alimentazione/agrumi-quali-sono-proprieta-nurizionali-e-possibili-controindicazioni.html "Agrumi: quali sono, proprietà nutrizionali e possibili controindicazioni") o ai frutti.
A piacere, prima di servire il dolce, distribuire della marmellata ai frutti rossi sulla superficie.
